Results

Each submission was reviewed by every member of the jury and received an initial score. All submission were then discussed and reevaluated in a discussion with all jury members which lead to a final score and selection of winners. The authors of the submissions were invitied to present their work virtually at the IEEE VIS conference 2024. Afterwards, the winning entries were announced.

Winning Entry

PlumeViz: Interactive Exploration for Multi-Facet Features of Hydrothermal Plumes in Sonar Images

Yiming Shao, Chengming Liu, Zhiyuan Meng, Shufan Qian, Peng Jiang, Yunhai Wang, Dr. Qiong Zeng
Shandong University, China & Renmin University of China

Additional Awards

Most Innovative Work

Topology Based Visualization of Hydrothermal Plumes

Adhitya Kamakshidasan, Harikrishnan Pattathil
Shell, Bangalore, India
