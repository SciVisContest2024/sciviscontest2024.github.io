2023 IEEE SciVis Contest

Neuronal Network Simulations of the Human Brain

Welcome to the 2023 IEEE SciVis Contest website. This contest is held annually as part of the IEEE VIS Conference.
In 2023, the conference and this contest will be held in Melbourne, Australia, and will focus on the visualization of
neuronal network simulations of plasticity changes in the human brain.

On this website, you can find information on the provided data, the visualization tasks, as well as the submission process.
If there still are questions, feel free to contact the contest chairs or join the Google Group. .

The deadline has been extended to August 7.
